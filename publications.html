Research Interests

  • Machine Learning
               - GANs, Generative AI, Deep learning, NLU, Explainable AI, Privacy-preserving Federated ML.
               - Information retrieval, Natural language processing, Text summarization.
  • Cryptography and Blockchain
               - Cryptocurrencies and Blockchain protocols (consensus, privacy, scalability, stability).
               - One-way functions, Zero-Knowledge proofs, AMMs, DeFi protocols, MPC, DKG.
               - Designing large-scale decentralized and privacy-preserving social Networks.
  • Game Theory, Computational Complexity
               - Derandomization, Space bounded computation.
               - Complexity aspects of games, equilibrium and fixed points.
  • Structural Graph theory
               - Treewidth, Minors, Digraph width parameters.
               - Matching, Pfaffians, Graceful labeling.
