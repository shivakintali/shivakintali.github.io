Paintings
I work across a variety of mediums, including acrylic paints, oil pastels, and intricate pencil and micro-liner pen sketches, rendered on wrapped canvas, canvas boards, gesso boards, and heavyweight paper. What truly distinguishes my art is the delightful touch of spontaneity infused into each piece — a captivating interplay of creative chaos that comes alive through expressive (brush, pastel, pencil, pen) strokes and richly textured palette-knife layers. My vibrant, multi-layered backgrounds pulse with energy. No two art pieces are ever the same.

All my paintings are original hand-drawn and hand-painted. They are varnished for extra protection. They are framed in high-quality frames, ready to hang.
